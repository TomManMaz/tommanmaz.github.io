Problem 12406 by R. Mortini and R. Rupp (Luxembourg/Germany)
For fixed \(p \in \mathbb{R}\), find all functions \(f : [0, 1] \to \mathbb{R}\) that are continuous at 0 and 1 and satisfy \[ f(x^2) + 2p \cdot f(x) = (x + p)^2 \] for all \(x \in [0, 1]\). 		PDF
